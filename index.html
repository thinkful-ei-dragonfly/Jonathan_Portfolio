I am a web developer and current student of the Engineering Immersion bootcamp at Thinkful. My initial career ambition was to purue a career as an academic philosopher. I've found that coding has given me the best of both worlds. When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing strategy games like chess or poker, training in Muay Thai or Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, and powerlifting. And on occasion, I'll read some analytic philosophy.
